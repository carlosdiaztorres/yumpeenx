Privacy Policy

At Yumpeen, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. We design digital experiences with integrity, and that extends to how we handle your data. This policy outlines exactly how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in plain language.

01

Data collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you request a project proposal, subscribe to our insights, or communicate with us via third-party platforms. This may include your name, email address, company details, and project budget.

Additionally, we automatically collect certain data when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and interaction metrics. This helps us optimize our digital performance and ensure security.

02

Use of information

The data we collect is used primarily to provide and improve our services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Process and manage your agency projects and service requests.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • Analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services to improve user experience.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.
03

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

cookie

Cookie preferences

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

04

Your rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data. You have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional data protection rights. Yumpeen aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data.
